Tools

The Prom 2021 AM have given you some tips to be successful in these 3 years of Overcome and in life in general.

Pomodoro Technique

It was my ally to be able to manage and take advantage of my time, so I can do my tasks and take care of my personal life. I usually spend 20 minutes and then a short break (5 minutes) and continued with my tasks successively.

Study in groups

Share ideas with my colleagues about a topic and try to analyze what we have learned. In that way, you will understand more.

Watch tv/series

in English audio and Spanish subtitles; then English audio and English subtitles; and finally, with no subtitles.

Never say you can't

even if everything goes wrong; Success is not easy.

Use a notebook

to write everything while you are learning programming and English.
